Privacy Policy – NeverLate

NeverLate only accesses your calendar data to provide reminders. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any other purposes.

Data We Access

  • Calendar events (to generate reminders)

How Data Is Used

Data is used only to schedule and display timely reminders within the extension.

Storage

Preferences and reminder settings are stored locally in your browser. No calendar data is stored outside your browser.

Third-Party Services

No third-party code is executed. Only Google Calendar API is used to fetch your events with your permission.
